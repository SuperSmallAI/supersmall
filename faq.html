What is SuperSmall.ai?

A 6-week intensive cohort where you'll learn to launch an AI-powered micro business. We teach practical AI skills, entrepreneurial fundamentals, and our SAGE framework (Skills, Attitude, Group support, Energy management)—everything you need to compete and thrive as a solo founder.

Who is this for?

Anyone ready to start their own business but unsure where to begin. No technical background required. Whether you're between jobs, making a career change, or finally ready to be your own boss—this program meets you where you are.

What makes this different from other courses?

Three things: (1) AI-first approach that lets you punch above your weight, (2) Live cohort with real accountability—not pre-recorded videos, (3) We address the whole founder journey: skills, mindset, community, and avoiding burnout.

Who teaches the program?

Seasoned entrepreneurs with successful exits and decades of experience building businesses. We've lived the challenges you're facing.

What will I learn?

Weeks 1-2: AI tools, idea validation, customer interviews, and building your landing page with vibe-coding

Weeks 3-4: Customer profiling, demand generation, lead generation tactics, and sales pipelines

Weeks 5-6: Entrepreneurial mindset, pricing strategy, launch planning, and your 90-day roadmap

You'll walk away with a validated idea, live website, prompt library, and action plan.

How much time does it take?

5-8 hours per week: 2 hours of live sessions (recorded if you miss them) plus 3-6 hours building and doing homework. This is hands-on—you create real business assets.

What's the format?

Live Zoom sessions twice weekly, capped at 12-15 participants for personalized attention. Sessions are recorded for catch-up.

What AI tools will I learn?

Practical tools you'll actually use: AI coding assistants (Cursor, v0), ChatGPT/Claude for content and strategy, design tools, automation platforms. Most have free tiers to start—budget $50-100/month if you want premium features.

Do I need technical experience?

Zero. If you can browse the web and use email, you have all the tech skills needed. We start from scratch.

What if I don't have a business idea?

Perfect—we start there. Week 1 covers idea generation and validation frameworks. You'll test multiple ideas quickly to find one with real market demand.

What types of businesses work best?

Service-based businesses, digital products, consulting, coaching, content creation, niche tools, online education—anything where AI helps you do the work of a larger team.

What equipment do I need?

Just a computer with internet, webcam, and microphone.

How does the application work?

1. Submit application + $50 fee
2. We review within 48 hours
3. If accepted, your $50 applies to tuition and we schedule a welcome call
4. Complete payment and you're in

Why charge an application fee?

The $50 ensures serious applicants and covers our review time. It's fully credited to tuition if accepted, fully refunded if not.

What's the total cost?

$1,495 total. Includes everything: live sessions, coaching, materials, community, and lifetime access.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. Attend the first two sessions and if it's not for you, we'll refund your tuition minus the $50 application fee. After week 2, sales are final.

When do cohorts start?

Quarterly. Current dates are on the application page. Cohorts are limited to 12-15 people—when full, you'll join the next cohort's priority list.

What will I have after 6 weeks?

• Validated business idea
• Live website you built
• Customized prompt library
• Lead generation system
• 90-day action plan
• Supportive founder community

Will I be making money?

Some participants land customers during the program, most within 30-60 days after. We give you everything needed to generate revenue—execution is up to you.

What support continues after graduation?

Lifetime access to the community, all materials, updated resources, and alumni network. Many grads continue collaborating and referring business to each other.

What if I fall behind?

All sessions are recorded and we have office hours. Life happens—we'd rather you finish strong than drop out.
