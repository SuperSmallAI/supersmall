Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical experience or coding skills?

Not at all. We designed this program for people with zero tech background. If you can use a web browser and follow instructions, you can build with AI. We start from the basics and get you confident fast.

What if I don't have a business idea yet?

Perfect—that's where we start. Week 1 focuses on idea generation and validation. You'll learn frameworks to test multiple ideas quickly and find one that actually has market demand before you commit.

How much time do I need each week?

Plan for 5-8 hours: 2 hours of live cohort sessions, plus time for homework and building. This is hands-on—you'll be creating real assets, not just watching videos.

What AI tools will I learn?

We focus on the tools that matter for micro businesses: AI coding assistants (like Lovable), content generation tools (ChatGPT), automation platforms, and research tools. You'll get our curated toolkit and learn exactly when to use each one.

Is this just another online course?

No. This is a live cohort with real-time feedback, community support, and accountability. You'll build alongside other founders, get direct access to experienced entrepreneurs, and leave with a launched product—not just certificates.

What happens after the 6 weeks?

You'll have a validated business idea, a live website, lead generation systems in place, and lifetime access to our community and resources. Many graduates continue collaborating and supporting each other long after the program ends.
